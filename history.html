The truffle is made of a high percentage water and minerals absorbed by the soil through the roots of the tree with which lives in symbiosis . It was created and develops near the roots of trees mainly those of the poplar , linden , oak and willow , becoming after the formation a real parasite. the characteristics coloring, flavor and aroma of truffles will be determined by the type of trees at which they will develop. for example truffles that grow near the oak trees have a scent more meaningful , while those near the lindens are more clear and aromatic . The shape , instead depend the type of soil : if the soft truffle is smoother , though compact , it will become knotty and lumpy due to the difficulty of get space .
