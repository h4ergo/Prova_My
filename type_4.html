Truffles Uncinato - Tuber Unicinatum Chatin


Features: This Truffle has a pleasant and delicate aroma reminiscent of Hazelnut. They have a dark, rough skin with large points, and a veined and light brown colored flesh. Thay are usually associated with oak, hazelnut and chestnut trees, growing in the tree's root.


Harvest in Abruzzo 21 September to 15 January (Region may extend period)
