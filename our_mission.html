Our Mission


The mission of My Italian Truffles is to provide the best truffle product, aromatic and clean, from the region of Abruzzo in keeping with the strict guidelines established by the region. And, therefore, introduce the Abruzzo Truffle to the USA market.


Like the symbiotic relation of the Truffle and its plant it is our mission to work in a close relationship with our customers.

Additionally it is the paramount goal of the cooperative to respect and nourish the protected nature reserves of the region.
