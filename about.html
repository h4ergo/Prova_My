About Us

Our Company


Our company is located in Abruzzo , the heart of Italy, between pristine land surrounded by green forests of the Apennines.
Define our products as "Gold Abruzzo " because we sell a unique product in the world, made available to us for all the best chefs in the world.
Our company from Abruzzo makes shipments of fresh truffles every day, the products are never kept more than a day outside of its natural environment. Our goal is the fragrance, what makes us the best choice.
