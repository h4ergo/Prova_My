Our Company



My Italian Truffles LLC is a United States based company, in New Jersey, currently doing business with food distributors (not available to the retail market) in Italy, United States of America, France, Germany and Switzerland, Dubai and Singapore. In Italy the company is represented by Marco Saccoccia, My Italian Truffles Italy.

Marco, who produces and distributes the finest Gelato throughout Abruzzo, Gelato Stella, has formed a Truffle Cooperative, much like the Wine Cooperatives in Abruzzo, in order to achieve the greatest returns from the collective efforts of the "Cavatori" (Truffle searches and harvesters).

As part of our brand and to differentiate ourselves in the marketplace we have our own Cavatori who have their own land, but more importantly are intimately familiar with the National Parks of Abruzzo where much of the White Truffles are found!


Finding White Truffles is literally a search in progress because they grow wild. Many attempts to cultivate the White Truffle, like the Black Truffle, have proven elusive hence the euphemisms, "Treasure" or "Diamonds of the kitchen." Therefore, each of our shipments is inspected by region officials and carries its own Certificato Fitosantario (Sanitary Certificate) individually signed.


As an additional part of our brand and to further differentiate our selves in the marketplace MY Italian Truffles will partner with our distributors to bring knowledgeable truffle professionals from Italy to meet and confer with you and the chefs whom you serve. Additionally we will bring an Italian chef, with products from Italy, to hold tasting and share recipes from Italy.
