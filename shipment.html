Shipment Methods and Countries served


Our products will arrive at your home through international express couriers, properly packed and covered by insurance if desired.

Shipping costs vary depending on the target area and the quantity of the goods. The amount of shipping is indicated directly on the budget required.

Our company is supported only by the best international shipping services. All our parcels are traceable at any time you can see the progress of the expedition.

Our shipments are made with all carriers certified by carriers at a controlled temperature.

The cargo is shipped in a container that never goes under and never rises above the optimum temperature for the storage of truffles. We like to keep the fragrance of the product and that is why we choose only the best for our customers.
