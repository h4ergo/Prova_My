Shipment

Shipment Methods and Countries served


All shipping is done with a shipping broker in Italy to the United States of America and coordinated with a shipping broker in the arrival city closest to the food distributor. The USA broker can also be one chosen by the food distributor.


Our product is harvested one day prior to packing and needs to spend minimum time in transit; therefore, shipping is done from Rome (Fiumicino Airport) and primarily on direct airline flights as cargo. Naturally allowances can be made due to airline operations or for cities not served by direct flights from Rome, Italy.

Additionally the product is shipped in temperature controlled boxes at 4 to 8 degrees centigrade. We are currently testing a devise that monitors the package temperature while in transit.

Currently we can ship to all US cities. Local European carriers are used for the EU (European Union) as needed and requested.
