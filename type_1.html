White Truffle - Tuber Magnatum Pico


Features: outside has a smooth yellow ocher or olive yellow. The inner flesh is yellowish white with brownish shades that vary according to the ripeness, the type of soil and the plant which lives in symbiosis, for example truffles that come close to an oak, a glebe are hazel, while those who grow close to the beech, are more reddish. It has a particularly strong and distinctive smell of cheese and garlic flavor, it may seem slightly spicy and vaguely similar to that of parmesan cheese.


Harvesting period: 1 October - 31 December
