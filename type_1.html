White Truffle - Tuber Magnatum Pico


Features: The White Truffle is considered the quintessential truffle because of its flavor and commercial value and importance. The outside has a yellow/olive color while the inner part is a yellowish white shade that varies according to the ripeness, soil and the surrounding tree. Pasta, eggs and risotto are enhanced and complimented by the White Truffle; you just will not be able to wait for the next fork full!


Harvest in Abruzzo: 1 October to 31 December (Region usually period extends to January)
